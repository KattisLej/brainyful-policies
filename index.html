Privacy policy for Brainyful apps

As required from Google Play all apps on Play need to have a privacy policy. The apps presented by Brainyful (Vabbis, DinnerSpinner and Aktivitetsväljaren) are all free apps with the purpose to help in the daily life in a simple and sometimes funny way. Brainyful is a one developer individual project with no profits or budget. These apps mostly serve as a portfolio. Feel free to try them out and let me know what you think! On this page you can read the provacy policies of those three apps that doesn't collect any personal or sensitive information.

Privacy policy for Vabbis

Vabbis is an app that will help you takes notes about your children sick leave. Find Vabbis on Google Play Store.

The app uses a local database meaning the notes that are entered in the app are only stored locally on the phone. There is no cloud back up or log in to retrieve these notes.

This app doesn't collect any personal or sensitive user data. Therefore, the app doesn't use, or share user data. There is no log in , no personal data such as email, phone number, name etc.

Privacy policy for Dinner Spinner

Dinner Spinner is an app that will randomise your dinner in an unexpected way but will give some inspiration. Find Dinner Spinner on Google Play Store.

This app doesn't collect any personal or sensitive user data. Therefore, the app doesn't use, or share user data. There is no log in , no personal data such as email, phone number, name etc.
